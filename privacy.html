Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

What We Collect

Ghost Runner collects GPS location data and fitness activity data (via HealthKit) solely to provide the running and ghost pacer features. This data is processed entirely on your device.

Data Storage

Your run history, routes, and lap data are stored locally on your iPhone. No data is sent to external servers. The app runs a local HTTP server on your phone to communicate with the Even Realities G2 glasses — this traffic stays on your device (localhost).

No Tracking

Ghost Runner does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or any form of user tracking. We do not collect, share, or sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

Ghost Runner uses Apple MapKit for route rendering. Map tile requests are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services are used.

Even Realities G2

The app communicates with Even Realities G2 smart glasses via the Even Hub SDK. Display data is sent over Bluetooth from your phone to your glasses. No data leaves your local device network.
